Frequently asked questions

Quick answers before you dive in.

How fast are books generated?

Images are generated sequentially for best quality. A 10-page storybook may take several minutes.

Where do my credits come from?

Credits are added automatically after your in-app purchase (Lite/Pro/Elite) via RevenueCat.

Can I edit pages?

Yes. Adjust prompts, attach reference images, and confirm each page before exporting.

What formats are supported?

Download a print-ready PDF. Images are in high-quality raster formats suitable for print.
